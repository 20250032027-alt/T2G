Our Story

About T2G

A Filipino company with deep roots in the coconut-growing heartland of Northern Mindanao.

The Company

Get in touch

Trunk to Gold (T2G) is based in Cagayan de Oro, Northern Mindanao, and has been producing premium coconut products since its founding. We are a producer first: we own our farms, run our own processing, and handle distribution ourselves. That full control is what lets us stand behind the quality of everything we make.

Our flagship product, T2G Coco Sugar, is made from fresh coconut sap that is minimally processed to preserve its natural minerals, vitamins, and amino acids. It has built a loyal following among health-conscious consumers and is now exported to buyers across Asia, the Middle East, Europe, and North America.

From the Source

Rooted in Northern Mindanao

Northern Mindanao is one of the Philippines' most productive coconut regions, and T2G is built on that foundation. Our plantations supply the raw material for every product we make, giving us direct oversight from tree to table.

We work with a small team of experienced agricultural staff and rely on centralized processing to keep quality consistent across every batch. Regular testing through internationally accredited labs (Intertek, SGS) backs up what we see on the ground.

What Drives Us

What we are here to do

We supply customers with the finest all-natural coconut food products from the Philippines. That sentence does not change depending on the order size or the market. The standard stays the same whether we are filling a local wholesale order or shipping a pallet to Europe.

We want T2G to be a Philippine brand that is recognized internationally for quality, traceability, and honesty. Not a company that just puts "natural" on the label because it sells well, but one where the label is the least interesting part because the product speaks for itself.

And we are committed to doing this in a way that makes a real difference for the farmers and communities in Northern Mindanao who make it possible.

People Behind It

A company that actually pays its farmers fairly

There is a lot of "farm-to-table" branding out there that does not survive a closer look. At T2G, the farms are ours. The farmers are people we know by name. We pay fair rates, maintain safe working conditions, and are invested in long-term relationships because our business only works if theirs does too.

When you buy T2G, you are supporting real livelihoods in Northern Mindanao, not just a label.

Why T2G

What makes us different

Farm ownership

We own our coconut plantations and run centralized processing. No third-party farms, no outsourced production, no guessing about what is in the supply chain.

Lab-tested quality

Every batch is tested by globally accredited labs including Intertek and SGS. We use ISO-certified packaging. The paperwork matches what is in the product.

Export experience

We have shipped to buyers in Asia, the Middle East, Europe, and North America. We know the logistics, the documentation, and how to get things there in good shape.

Low glycemic index

T2G Coco Sugar has a GI of 35. That is not a marketing claim; it is a tested figure. It makes a real difference for diabetics and anyone watching their blood sugar.

Fair to farmers

We pay our farmers fairly because the alternative is a fragile supply chain built on underpaid labor, and that is not what T2G is about.

No additives

No artificial flavors, no preservatives, no colors. Minimal processing means the coconut does most of the work, and we just get out of the way.

Trunk to Gold produces premium, all-natural coconut products from Cagayan de Oro, Northern Mindanao. From our farms to your table.
