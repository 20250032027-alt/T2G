Who We Are

The Company

Trunk to Gold (T2G) is a proudly Filipino company based in Cagayan de Oro, Northern Mindanao — the coconut heartland of the Philippines. We are dedicated to producing, marketing, and exporting premium, all-natural coconut by-products that promote health, well-being, and sustainable livelihoods.

Our flagship product — T2G Coco Sugar — has grown to become a trusted name among health-conscious consumers who demand purity and quality without compromise.

Everything we produce starts at the source: our own coconut plantations, where generations of agricultural knowledge meet modern food safety practices.

Our Purpose

Mission & Vision

🎯

Our Mission

We are committed to supplying our customers with the finest, all-natural coconut food products produced in the Philippines — from our farms to your table, with integrity and care at every step.

Through our efforts, we are dedicated to making a positive difference in the lives of our employees, farmers, and the communities we serve.

🌟

Our Vision

We strive to be a globally recognized Philippine brand known for its world-class coconut products, uncompromising quality, and deep respect for food safety and natural goodness.

We envision a world where the coconut palm's full potential is celebrated and accessible to everyone, everywhere.

Our Advantage

Why T2G?

🌾

Our Experience

Our production team includes agricultural engineers with deep expertise in Philippine coconut farming. We are pioneers in commercializing coconut sugar production in Northern Mindanao, with over a decade of refined practice.

🏅

Our Quality

We own our coconut plantations and employ centralized processing to ensure consistent quality and food safety at every batch. Regular testing is conducted by globally accredited international labs (Intertek, SGS), and we use ISO-certified packaging.

🌍

Our Reach

T2G products are available in select Philippine retail outlets, health stores, restaurants, and cafés. We are also an experienced exporter to markets across Asia, the Middle East, Europe, the United States, and Canada.

Our People

Rooted in Community

Behind every jar and pouch is a community of hardworking coconut farmers, processors, and packaging staff from Northern Mindanao. We believe in fair wages, safe working conditions, and long-term relationships with the people who make T2G possible.

When you choose T2G, you're not just choosing a healthier product — you're supporting Filipino livelihoods and sustainable farming practices.
