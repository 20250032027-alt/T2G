The Company

Get in touch

Trunk to Gold (T2G) is a Filipino producer based in Cagayan de Oro, Northern Mindanao, dedicated to bringing premium, all-natural coconut products to families and businesses around the world.

Our flagship product, T2G Coco Sugar, is made from fresh coconut sap with minimal processing, keeping its natural nutrients intact. It has grown into a trusted name among health-conscious consumers who care about what goes into their food.

From Our Farms

We own the whole process, start to finish

Our coconut plantations are in Northern Mindanao, one of the Philippines' richest coconut-growing regions. We handle everything from harvest to packaging in-house, which keeps quality consistent and traceable from the tree to the shelf.

It also means we work directly with the farmers and communities who make this possible, paying fair rates and investing in the people behind every jar.
