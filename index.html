Who We Are

Rooted in the Philippine Coconut Heartland

T2G — Trunk to Gold is a passionate producer of premium coconut-based food products from Northern Mindanao. Every product we create starts at the source: our own coconut farms, where generations of knowledge meet modern food safety standards.

Our mission is simple — deliver the purest, healthiest coconut products to Filipino families and international markets alike, while supporting our local farming communities.

Our Promise

Why Choose T2G?

🌴

Farm-to-Table

We own our coconut farms and control every step of production — from harvest to packaging — ensuring the freshest, most consistent quality.

🌿

100% All-Natural

No artificial additives, no preservatives. Our products are minimally processed to retain the full spectrum of natural nutrients and flavors.

🏅

Quality Certified

Regular testing by internationally accredited laboratories. ISO-certified packaging. We meet the highest global food safety standards.

🚢

Export-Ready

Experienced exporter to Asia, the Middle East, Europe, the US, and Canada. Private label and custom packaging available on request.

🤝

Farmer-First

We work directly with local coconut farmers, ensuring fair compensation and sustainable livelihoods across Northern Mindanao.

💛

Low Glycemic

Our coconut sugar and syrup carry a low GI of 35 — a smarter, healthier alternative for diabetics and the health-conscious.
