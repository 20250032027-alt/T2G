How It Works

Export terms and process

Product Samples

We offer free product samples. You cover the courier cost.

If you have a courier account with DHL or FedEx, send us the details and we will arrange a collect shipment. If not, we can get a quote on your behalf.

Lead Time

Loose cargo orders typically take 2 to 6 weeks from confirmed payment, depending on order size and current queue.

Production starts after full or agreed down payment is received.

Packaging Specifications

All products are packaged and labelled to their given specs. Spec sheets available on request.

LCL sea freight pallet: 115 x 115 x 189 cm (H), approximately 2.5 CBM.

Minimum Order Quantity

MOQ: 1 to 2 pallets, mixed or per variant.

Discounted rates for larger orders. Email trunk2gold@gmail.com for a formal quote.

Payment Terms

2 pallets or fewer: 100% payment 5 days before shipment.

More than 2 pallets: 50% down payment, balance 5 days before shipment.

Preferred method: wire transfer.

Private Label

We are open to private label. If you want to sell T2G products under your own brand, get in touch and we can talk through what that looks like.

Email us at trunk2gold@gmail.com to start the conversation.
