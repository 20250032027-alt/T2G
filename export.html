How It Works

Export Information

📦

Product Samples

We offer free product samples — you only cover the courier cost. If you need more than a standard sample set, let us know and we can arrange accordingly.

If you already have a courier account (DHL, FedEx, etc.), provide the details for a collect shipment. We can also get a quote on your behalf.

⏱️

Lead Time

Loose cargo orders typically take 2–6 weeks, depending on the order quantity and our current production queue.

Orders are confirmed and production begins after full or down payment is received as agreed upon.

📐

Packaging Specifications

All products are packaged and labelled to their given specifications. Detailed spec sheets are available upon request.

LCL Pallet Specifications (Sea Freight):
Pallet Size: 115 × 115 × 189 cm (H), approx. 2.5 CBM

📊

Minimum Order Quantity (MOQ)

T2G Products: 1–2 pallets (mixed or per variant).

Discounted rates are available for larger orders. For a formal quote, email us at trunk2gold@gmail.com.

💳

Payment Terms

Orders ≤ 2 pallets: 100% payment, 5 days before shipment.

Orders > 2 pallets: 50% down payment, balance 5 days before shipment.

Preferred payment method: Wire Transfer (T/T).

🏷️

Private Label

We are open to private label arrangements. If you're interested in selling T2G products under your own brand, we'd love to discuss the possibilities.

Reach out to us at trunk2gold@gmail.com to start the conversation.
