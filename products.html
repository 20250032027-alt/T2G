Virgin Coco Oil

Cold-Pressed Unrefined Rich in MCTs

Extracted from fresh coconut meat through a cold-press or traditional fermentation process, our Virgin Coco Oil retains its natural aroma, flavor, and full nutritional profile — including beneficial medium-chain triglycerides (MCTs).

Unlike refined coconut oil, our VCO undergoes minimal processing with no solvents, bleaching, or deodorizing — giving you pure, unadulterated coconut goodness.
