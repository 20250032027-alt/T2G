Virgin Coco Oil

Cold-Pressed Unrefined Rich in MCTs

Pressed from fresh coconut meat without refinement, bleaching, or deodorizing. The natural aroma, flavor, and full nutritional profile stay intact, including medium-chain triglycerides (MCTs).

This is different from regular coconut oil, which uses solvents and processing to maximize extraction at the cost of everything that makes coconut oil worth using.
